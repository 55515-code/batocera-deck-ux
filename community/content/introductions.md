# Introduce yourself

Tell the community what brought you here and what you would enjoy helping with.
You might mention your handheld or Linux experience, a game or workflow you care
about, and whether you are interested in development, testing, art and UX,
documentation, packaging, repair, or preservation.

Newcomers are welcome. You do not need a contributor title or specialist knowledge
to ask thoughtful questions, report a reproducible problem, or improve an unclear
piece of documentation.
