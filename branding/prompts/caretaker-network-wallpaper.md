# Caretaker Network Wallpaper

The production master was generated with the built-in OpenAI image-generation
tool on 2026-07-19. The supplied LuigiOS art-guidance boards were used only as
mood, palette, and composition references. They are not production assets.

## Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept

Asset type: production desktop wallpaper master for LuigiOS, 16:10
handheld-first composition that also crops safely to 16:9 television

Primary request: create an original, calm science-fiction landscape expressing
community stewardship, repair, preservation, and a peer-to-peer network. A
small original raccoon caretaker in practical, unbranded field gear sits at the
lower right tending a compact glowing data lantern beside modular storage
cases. Across a distant dark valley, sparse green route lights connect small
community shelters toward one abstract upright geometric monolith shaped only
as a simple angular L made from two rectangular beams.

Style: polished original digital environment painting, restrained
retro-futurist field-guide sensibility, crisp enough for an operating-system
wallpaper, not photorealistic and not cartoonish.

Composition: wide 16:10, with generous quiet near-black negative space across
the upper left and center for desktop UI. Keep the focal character and gear in
the lower-right quarter, the horizon low, and all important details inside a
72px television-safe margin.

Palette: OLED black `#000000`, charcoal `#121416` and `#262626`, restrained
accents `#22C55E` and `#66FF66`, and pale neutral highlights.

Constraints: wholly original mascot and environment; generic portable-computing
equipment with no protected product trade dress; no text, logo, watermark, UI,
game art, Nintendo or Mario features, penguins, Tux, Steam Deck silhouette,
vendor controllers, crypto imagery, armor, weapons, neon overload, purple-blue
gradient, bokeh, blanket green tint, or childish mascot energy.
